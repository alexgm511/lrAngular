• Does the LandRider come fully assembled?
    The LandRider comes 95% assembled! Once you've removed the packing material, you can assemble your LandRider in less than 15 minutes using the enclosed multi-tool! Simply insert the seat and handlebars into the frame, screw on the pedals, attach the front wheel and you are ready to ride!

  • Does the LandRider require special maintenance?
    As with any bicycle, you should have it serviced regularly to prevent damage or unnecessary wear and tear on the moving mechanical parts. Most bike shops charge $30 to $40 for a complete tune-up. See Owner's Manual for service recommendations.
